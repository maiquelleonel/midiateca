[Partido Pirata] PPI Conference: statutes amendments and board candidatures

Pirate Party International Tue, 01 Feb 2011 13:20:28 -0800 

Dear Pirates,

As you know, from March 12th to 13th 2011 the first regular PPI conference
will take place in Friedrichshafen, Germany.

During this conference, the General Assembly will decide and vote, among
other things, on amendments to the PPI statutes and elect a new board.

Each member is permitted to submit amendments to the PPI statutes. The
current version of the PPI statutes can be found here:
http://int.piratenpartei.de/Statutes (English, Italian, Czech, preliminary
versions of German and Russian). To submit your proposals please use this
form: http://piraten.in/statutesform  . The form also allows for non-members
to submit proposals for  amendments. The deadline for submitting proposals
is February 12th 2011,  23:59  CET.
The statutes taskforce will collect the proposals, prepare a document will
all the proposed statutes amendments and publish it at the latest one week
before the conference (5th March 2011).

The General Assembly will also elect a new board of the PPI. Candidates
shall submit their application on
http://int.piratenpartei.de/PPI_Conference_2011/Board_candidatures or e-mail
their application *to bo...@pp-international.net*.

For further information concerning the 2011 PPI conference you can visit
http://int.piratenpartei.de/PPI_Conference_2011 or contact the board at
bo...@pp-international.net.

the Board of the PPI
