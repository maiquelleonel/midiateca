http://www.facebook.com/topic.php?uid=108734602494994&topic=5164

Dear WikiLeaks supporters,
We are creating a video highlighting how WikiLeaks releases have helped produce positive changes in countries around the world.
We would like to include a number of subjects from different countries, talking honestly in YouTube self-shot style about your thoughts.
If you would like to submit a video, please record yourself discussing the following points in a short video, post this on YouTube, and post the video URL (web address) to our Facebook group.
Video URLs must be posted to the "youtube videos here" topic in the Discussions tab.
Please plan a concise dialogue, and keep the video to no longer than 3 minutes. 

. Introduce yourself (your name, country of residence, and profession).
. Describe concisely one positive change WikiLeaks has caused, or is causing in your country, and why it is important. 

. A quick message of support to the WikiLeaks team.
We would like as many videos as possible, but the deadline for this intake is Tuesday 25th of January. If you want your voice included, please send ASAP!
By sending us the URL of your video to our Facebook group you agree to contribute this video to the public domain. 

If you can help us with a donation, please follow this link:
http://wikileaks.ch/Support.html

Many thanks for your support,

The WikiLeaks Team
