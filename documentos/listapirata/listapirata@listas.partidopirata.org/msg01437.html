[Partido Pirata] Support the PPI Conference with your donation!

Pirate Party International Sun, 23 Jan 2011 17:21:48 -0800 

Dear Pirates,

>From March 12th to 13th. 2011 the first regular PPI conference will take
place in Friedrichshafen, Germany.

As the PPI collects no membership fees, the organizing committee relies on
participation fees and donations to organize the event. Therefore we call
for your support of this year's PPI conference. If you want to donate,
you'll find all the necessary information on this page:
http://int.piratenpartei.de/PPI_Conference_2011#Support and at the end of
this message. Your money will be used to pay for the venue, the technical
equipment, sponsor of Pirates who cannot pay the participation fee,
catering, social events etc. Should the donations exceed the amount needed
for the PPI conference, they can be reimbursed.

If you think you know any potential sponsors please invite them to
participate in the conference sponsoring, or write us an E-Mail with your
ideas and we will contact them. Sponsors can register on this page by
filling out the sponsors section:
http://conference.pp-international.net/PPI/conference2010.nsf/faqs.xsp

For further information concerning the 2011 PPI conference you can visit
http://int.piratenpartei.de/PPI_Conference_2011 or contact the board at
bo...@pp-international.net.

Thank you for your support!

Don't forget: guests are more than welcome! So if you want to participate in
the PPI conference register here:
http://conference.pp-international.net/PPI/conference2010.nsf/registration.xsp

the Board of the PPI
